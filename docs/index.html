Menu bar at a glance

Color-coded percentage in the top bar. Green, yellow, orange, red — you always know how much room you have left.

Exact reset times

No more fuzzy “about an hour.” Headroom shows the precise reset timestamp and a live countdown down to the second.

Smart notifications

Get warned at 75%, 85%, 90%, and 95%. When a window resets, you get a notification — only for the limit that actually reset.

Private by design

Your session cookie stays in the macOS Keychain. No servers, no tracking, no accounts. Just you and the Claude usage API.

Setup in under a minute

  1. Download and open Headroom.
  2. Copy your sessionKey cookie from claude.ai.
  3. Paste it in Settings. Done.
